How do I use WebMDRx to save on prescription drugs?

Saving money on your prescriptions with WebMDRx is as easy as showing your WebMDRx coupon at the pharmacy counter. You may print it out or display it on your phone.

With WebMDRx, simply:

  1. Search for your prescription and compare costs at pharmacies near you.
  2. Confirm the quantity, dosage, and form of your prescription.
  3. Print the coupon from the website, have it emailed to you, or have it sent to your smartphone.

How do I make sure my prescription dosage, quantity, or brand is properly selected?

When you enter your prescription, we will display the most commonly prescribed quantity, form, and dosage. WebMDRx will show you generic prescriptions over brand names, as they are often less costly. If you need to make changes to the pre-populated dosage, quantity, or brand, you can do so by selecting "Edit Preferences" on your prescriptions results page.

Simply select the options that match your prescription, and the page will reload to show your savings that match your new prescription details.​

How do I transfer a prescription?

Every pharmacy is different when it comes to transferring your prescriptions. Some let you complete the transfer online, while others require you to call them. Either way, you will need to provide the new pharmacy with some basic information:

  1. Your name
  2. The prescription information
  3. The name and phone number of your previous pharmacy

The new pharmacy will then call your previous pharmacy and have your prescriptions transferred. While it varies from pharmacy to pharmacy, the transfer can take anywhere from a few hours to a couple of days to complete. (You can provide your WebMDRx information over the phone or at the counter when you pick up your prescription.)

What is the Pharmacy Regular Price (Reg.)?

The regular price is an estimated amount that will be charged by the pharmacy to a person who does not have any prescription insurance or coupons, commonly referred to as the cash price. The regular price is averaged using submitted data, including usual and customary price, from recent transactions at the pharmacy or chain. The regular price is not guaranteed, is subject to change, may vary at the time of purchase due to inventory availability and other factors, and may be inaccurate. The regular price should only be used as a guideline to calculate possible savings with the program.
